Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 tablepoon of butter
  • White wine(anything would work)
  • 1/8 t salt and black pepper
  • pinch of saffron
  • chicken stock
  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • chopped white onion
  • 1 c broken walnuts (optional)

Instructions

place a pot with no lid to a medium temperature.

add butter into pot and wait for it to melt.

add white onion and let it saute.

add rice into pot and let it warm up.

Add white wine and let the alchohol evaporate.

have chicken broth warm before cooking rice and mix it in a 50/50 ratio with warm water, add slowely to pot and stir.

slowely add chicken stock/water until the rice is cooked it may take up to 30 miuntes if cooking at a slow temperature.

Most important step is stirring the pot to make sure the rice, onion, butter, pot itself burns without any liqued to absorb the heat.
